What is Fleetarr?

Fleetarr is a native app for iOS, iPadOS and macOS that gives you an at-a-glance status and activity dashboard across your self-hosted media automation stack – Sonarr, Radarr, Prowlarr, Seerr, SABnzbd, Plex and Jellyfin. See what’s healthy, what needs attention, and clear the small, frequent problems right from your phone.

Features

  • Combined fleet health with a single at-a-glance problem count
  • Per-service detail: download queues, indexer status, pending requests, active streams, calendar and history
  • Quick actions – approve a request, remove a stuck download, stop a runaway stream, retry a failed grab
  • Works over Tailscale / WireGuard / LAN, with self-signed certs and reverse-proxy sub-paths
  • Your setup syncs across your devices via iCloud, end-to-end encrypted – keys stay in iCloud Keychain
  • Native and adaptive: light & dark, Dynamic Type, VoiceOver, Home Screen widget and macOS menu bar

Available for iOS 17+, iPadOS 17+ and macOS 14+.

Privacy

Fleetarr talks only to your own services and keeps its configuration on your own devices. Your API keys stay in iCloud Keychain (end-to-end encrypted), and usage analytics are fully anonymous. Details are in the Privacy Policy.
